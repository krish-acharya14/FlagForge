# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the FlagForge community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race or religion.

Our goal is to foster an open, friendly, and collaborative environment where everyone feels comfortable contributing.

---

# Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

* Being respectful and welcoming.
* Giving constructive feedback.
* Being open to different viewpoints.
* Helping newcomers.
* Respecting differing opinions.
* Focusing on what is best for the community.
* Showing empathy toward others.

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Trolling or deliberately provoking others.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Offensive or inappropriate language.
* Threats or intimidation.
* Spam or disruptive behavior.

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate these guidelines.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the project.

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through GitHub.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Project documentation
* Community interactions related to FlagForge

---
